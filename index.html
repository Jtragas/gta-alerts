Product plan

What GTA Alerts will do

Custom alert zones

Users can set a radius around home, school, work, current location, or a commute route.

Official public feeds

Fire, transit, roads, traffic cameras, weather, and delayed public police calls where available.

Waze-style reports

Community reports can be marked unverified, verified, or confirmed by public data.

Walking, driving, transit

Different alert modes for pedestrians, drivers, transit riders, families, and city-wide watchers.
